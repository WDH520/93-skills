- NotoSansCJKsc-Regular.otf: 常规字重 (Regular, 15.68 MB)
- NotoSansCJKsc-Bold.otf: 粗体 (Bold, 16.21 MB)

思源黑体 (Noto Sans CJK) 是 Adobe 和 Google 联合开发的开源泛中日韩字体，
支持简体中文、繁体中文、日文和韩文。

Source Han Sans (Noto Sans CJK) is an open source Pan-CJK font developed
by Adobe and Google, supporting Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese,
Japanese, and Korean.
